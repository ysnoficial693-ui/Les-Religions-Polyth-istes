Cristiano Ronaldo at Juventus

Cristiano Ronaldo joined Juventus in 2018 after an exceptional career at Real Madrid. During his time in Turin, he scored over 100 goals and won several Serie A titles, cementing his legendary status as one of the greatest footballers of all time. His influence on the team and his exemplary discipline inspired everyone at Juventus.
