CR7 – Career at Real Madrid

After his years at Manchester United, Cristiano Ronaldo joined Real Madrid in 2009, where he became a true legend. He has won numerous trophies, including several Champions Leagues, and is the club's all-time leading scorer.

CR7 has marked the history of football with his relentless work ethic, discipline, and determination. His exploits continue to inspire millions of fans around the world.
