CR7's Debut at Manchester United

Cristiano Ronaldo joined Manchester United in 2003 from Sporting Lisbon. He quickly showcased his incredible talent and determination on the pitch. His rapid dribbling, powerful shots, and ability to change the course of a match made him one of the most promising players of his generation.

Under the guidance of Sir Alex Ferguson, Ronaldo transformed into a global superstar, winning numerous trophies and setting legendary records. His Manchester debut marked the beginning of an exceptional career.
